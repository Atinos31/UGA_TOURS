About Us

Welcome to Uga Tours!

Uga tours is an award-winning safari travel company creating exceptional African Safari experiences since 1998. Based in kampala,Uganda,
We are dedicated to providing expert, independent advice, and 5-star service to our clients from the early planning phase until well after you return home. We travel the length and breadth of every destination we recommend to make sure that our expertise is based on first-hand experience and that every part of your itinerary is tried-and-tested.
